Senior Data Engineer

We are looking for a Senior Data Engineer with strong experience designing and building cloud-native batch and streaming data pipelines. The ideal candidate has hands-on experience with Python, SQL, Spark, Databricks, orchestration, data warehousing, data lake patterns, CI/CD, data quality, and governance.

Responsibilities include building reliable ETL/ELT workflows, ingesting structured and semi-structured data, optimizing warehouse models, supporting analytics and machine learning data products, implementing observability, and collaborating with product and analytics partners.

Preferred skills include AWS Glue, S3, Redshift, Lambda, Step Functions, Kinesis, Snowflake, Airflow, dbt, Kafka, Docker, GitHub Actions, Terraform, Great Expectations, and Power BI.
